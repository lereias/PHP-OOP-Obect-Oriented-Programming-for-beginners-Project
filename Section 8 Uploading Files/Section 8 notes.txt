php.ini configurations to focused on in uploading files
~ file_uploads		= True, 1, or On
~ post_max_size		= 8M (or any size, our size is 40M)
~ upload_max_filesize	= 2M (or any size, our size is 128M)
~ memory_limit		= 128M (or any size, our size is 512M)
~ max_input_time		= -1 or no limit (or any time, our time is 60s)
~ upload_tmp_dir		= NULL (or any upload_tmp_dir, our is C:\xampp\tmp)
~ max_execution_time	= 30s (or any time, our time is 120s)

to check where it is displayed, go to localhost/xampp/dashboard/phpinfo.php

or create a file named phpinfo.php in basic_files folder
<?php

phpinfo();

?>

to change the configurations, open the php.ini file, in this case
xampp/php/php.ini and find and change what you want. Changes will be
displayed in locations said earlier
